## Source

- URL: https://example.com/industry-report-2025
- File: `source/internal-data.csv`
- Reference: Company strategy document (Q4 2024)

## Request

Research and synthesize information about market trends in [topic area].
Compare external sources with internal data to identify:
- Gaps between market trends and current strategy
- Opportunities for growth
- Potential risks or threats

## Output

- **Format**: Markdown report with tables
- **Length**: 2-3 pages
- **Audience**: Strategy team
- **Filename**: market-trends-analysis.md

## Notes

- Include source citations
- Quantify findings where possible
- Flag any conflicting information between sources
